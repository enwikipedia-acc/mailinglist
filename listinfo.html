Accounts-enwiki-l Mailing List

About

This is a private mailing list is for Internal discussion between the English Wikipedia's account creation team.

To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the Archives

How to post to

To post a message to all the list members, send email to accounts (-) enwiki (-) l (at) lists (dot) wikimedia (dot) org.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.

All e-mails you send to this mailing list will be available on private web archives, on Wikimedia servers.

Subscribing to

Subscribing to this list is currently disabled. Please contact the list owner at accounts-enwiki-l-owner a t lists.wikimedia.org if you need to subscribe.

Changing options and unsubscribing

To change your mailing list options (such as to turn off mailings) or to unsubscribe completely from the mailing list, please log in with your e-mail address below.

You will be asked to provide your mailing list password. If cannot remember it, you will be able to request a password reminder.

If you leave the field blank, you will be prompted for your email address.

Administrators and Moderators

The Accounts-enwiki-l mailing list administrators and moderators can be contacted collectively by e-mail at accounts-enwiki-l-owner a t lists.wikimedia.org

Please only use the above email address to reach the mailing list administrators and moderators. If you wish to reach the ACC tool admins (which is a superset of the list administrators and moderators), please send your email instead to enwiki-acc-admins a t googlegroups.com
